Eric is a Choctaw native speaker, and the best friend of Dennis. He is growing up in a Choctaw speaking home that has strong beliefs in maintaining their Choctaw culture. The particpate in Choctaw church and cummunity events as often as possible.

Eric is taking the Choctaw language classes with Dennis for an Easy 'A' and with knowledge of the language, he is acting as a classroom mentor and tutor for the other students.

He wants to find an app that he can show to other classmates for practice so that he can have more time to focus on his own school work. 

Eric finds that most online tools are just lists of words which doesn't really help anyone learn the language and that even though he speaks the language fluently, he still has to look up words in the dictionary from time to time.
